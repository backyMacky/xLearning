Common Support Topics

Quick answers to our most frequently asked questions

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your email.

How do I verify my email address?

After registration, a verification link is sent to your email. Click the link to verify your account. If you didn't receive it, you can request a new verification link from the "Verify Email" page.

How do I update my profile information?

Log in to your account, click on your profile picture in the top-right corner, and select "My Profile." Then click "Edit Profile" to update your information.

Can I change my username?

Yes, you can change your username from your profile settings. Log in, go to "Edit Profile," and update your username field.

How do I apply to become a teacher?

Go to your profile and select "Request Teacher Status." Fill out the application form with your qualifications and teaching experience.

What are the requirements to become a teacher?

You should have fluency in the language you want to teach, some teaching experience, and a verified xLearning account.

How long does the approval process take?

The teacher approval process typically takes 2-3 business days. You'll receive a notification when your application is reviewed.

What happens if my application is rejected?

If your application is rejected, you'll receive feedback on why. You can improve your application based on the feedback and reapply after 30 days.

How secure is my personal information?

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. Your personal information is only used for the purposes outlined in our privacy policy.

Can I enable two-factor authentication?

This feature is coming soon. In the meantime, we recommend using a strong, unique password for your account.

Who can see my profile information?

Your public profile is visible to other users on the platform. You can control what information is displayed in your privacy settings.

How do I report a security concern?

If you have any security concerns or notice suspicious activity, please contact our support team immediately using the contact form below.

How does the credit system work?

Credits are the currency used on xLearning to book sessions with teachers. You can purchase credits and use them to book lessons.

How do I purchase credits?

Go to the Credits page from your dashboard, select a credit package, and complete the checkout process using your preferred payment method.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers depending on your region.

Can I get a refund for unused credits?

Yes, you can request a refund for unused credits within 30 days of purchase. Contact our support team to process your refund.
