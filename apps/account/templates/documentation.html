Account Management Guide

Managing Your User Profile

Your profile on xLearning contains important information about your account and learning preferences. Keeping this information up-to-date ensures the best experience on our platform.

Accessing Your Profile

To access your profile settings:

  1. Log in to your xLearning account
  2. Click on your profile picture in the top-right corner
  3. Select "My Profile" from the dropdown menu
  4. On your profile page, click "Edit Profile" to make changes

Profile Information You Can Update

From your profile settings, you can update the following information:

  • Profile Picture: Upload a photo to personalize your account
  • Personal Information: Update your name, username, and email address
  • Language Settings: Set your native language and the language you're learning
  • Contact Information: Update your phone number and date of birth
  • Bio: Add information about yourself that teachers and other students can see
Pro Tip

Adding a complete profile with a photo helps teachers get to know you better and can enhance your learning experience!

Teacher Application Process

Becoming a Teacher on xLearning

If you have language teaching experience and want to share your expertise with students worldwide, you can apply to become a teacher on xLearning.

Eligibility Requirements

To become a teacher on xLearning, you should have:

  • Fluency in the language you want to teach
  • Teaching experience (formal or informal)
  • A verified xLearning account
  • The ability to conduct online classes

Application Steps

  1. Submit Application: Go to your profile and select "Request Teacher Status"
  2. Provide Information: Fill out the form with your qualifications, teaching experience, and the subjects you want to teach
  3. Application Review: Our administration team will review your application
  4. Approval Process: If approved, you'll receive a notification and your account will be upgraded to teacher status

After Approval

Once approved as a teacher, you'll gain access to:

  • Teacher dashboard with course creation tools
  • Student management features
  • Scheduling and calendar functionalities
  • Payment processing for your classes
Important Note

The teacher approval process typically takes 2-3 business days. You'll receive a notification when your application is reviewed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common documentation questions

To reset your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address and follow the instructions sent to your email. You'll receive a link to create a new password. This link is valid for 24 hours.

After registration, a verification link is sent to your email address. Simply click on this link to verify your account. If you didn't receive the email, you can request a new verification link from the "Verify Email" page. Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't see the email in your inbox.

The teacher approval process typically takes 2-3 business days. Our administration team reviews each application carefully to ensure all teachers meet our quality standards. You'll receive a notification when your application has been reviewed, regardless of the outcome.

To update your notification preferences, go to your Account Settings and select the "Preferences" tab. From there, you can customize which notifications you receive via email and in-app. You can toggle notifications for messages, course updates, reminders, and promotional content.

xLearning has three main user types: Students, Teachers, and Administrators. Each has different permissions and capabilities. Additionally, administrators can create custom roles with specific permission sets. Check the Roles & Permissions section in the Administration documentation for full details on available permissions.
