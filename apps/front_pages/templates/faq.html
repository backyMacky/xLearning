General Questions

xLearning is a comprehensive platform designed specifically for language teachers. It provides tools for content management, live teaching sessions, student progress tracking, assessment creation, and more. Our platform helps language teachers streamline their workflows, engage students more effectively, and create a more organized teaching environment.

xLearning is designed for language teachers of all types, including:
  • Independent language tutors
  • Language school instructors
  • University language professors
  • Corporate language trainers
  • Online language teachers
Whether you teach one-on-one or manage multiple classes, xLearning can be customized to fit your teaching style and needs.

xLearning supports teaching in any language. The platform interface is available in English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, Arabic, and Russian. We provide special character support, right-to-left text direction, and language-specific tools for major languages. Our content management system can handle files in any language, and our assessment tools support language-specific question types.

Getting started is easy:
  1. Sign up for a free 14-day trial (no credit card required)
  2. Complete your profile and teaching preferences
  3. Use our setup wizard to configure your teaching environment
  4. Upload or create your first teaching materials
  5. Add your students and start teaching!
We also offer free onboarding sessions for new users, and our help center provides detailed guides and tutorials.

Yes, xLearning offers mobile apps for both iOS and Android devices. The mobile apps allow teachers to manage their courses, communicate with students, and access teaching materials on the go. Students can also use the mobile app to access course content, complete assignments, and participate in learning activities. All your data syncs seamlessly between the web and mobile platforms.

Platform Features

xLearning supports a wide range of content formats to accommodate all your teaching materials:
  • Documents: PDF, DOCX, DOC, TXT, RTF
  • Presentations: PPT, PPTX, Google Slides
  • Audio: MP3, WAV, M4A
  • Video: MP4, MOV, AVI, WebM
  • Images: JPG, PNG, GIF, SVG
  • Interactive: H5P, SCORM packages
  • Other: Google Drive files, OneDrive files, Dropbox files
You can also create content directly in our platform using our rich text editor, which supports embedding, interactive elements, and more.

Our assessment system offers multiple question types specifically designed for language learning:
  • Multiple choice questions
  • Fill in the blanks
  • Matching exercises
  • Sentence ordering
  • Audio recording responses
  • Essay and free text responses
  • Translation exercises
You can create quizzes, tests, and assignments, set time limits, randomize questions, and provide automatic or manual grading. Results are tracked in the student progress system, and you can provide feedback directly on assessments.

Yes, xLearning offers built-in video conferencing for live lessons with up to 25 participants. Our video platform includes features specifically designed for language teaching, such as:
  • Screen sharing and whiteboard
  • Breakout rooms for small group practice
  • Live chat with translation support
  • Recording capabilities for later review
  • Low latency for clear pronunciation
You can also integrate with Zoom, Google Meet, or Microsoft Teams if you prefer to use those platforms.

Our scheduling system allows you to:
  • Set your availability with customizable time slots
  • Allow students to book sessions within your available times
  • Create recurring sessions for regular classes
  • Send automatic reminders to students
  • Handle time zone differences automatically
  • Sync with your Google, Outlook, or Apple calendar
  • Track attended and missed sessions
The system also integrates with our video conferencing and payment features for a seamless experience.

Yes, xLearning provides comprehensive student progress tracking:
  • Track completion of lessons and activities
  • Monitor assessment scores and improvement over time
  • View time spent on different learning activities
  • Generate progress reports for individual students or groups
  • Set learning goals and track achievement
  • Identify areas where students may need additional support
Students can also access their own progress data, which helps increase engagement and motivation.

Account Questions

Creating an account is simple: click the "Start Free Trial" or "Register" button on our website, enter your email address and create a password, then follow the setup wizard to configure your teaching profile. You can also sign up using your Google or Microsoft account for quicker access. No credit card is required to create an account and start your 14-day free trial.

There are several ways to add students:
  1. Invite by email: Send invitation emails directly from the platform
  2. Enrollment link: Create a custom link that students can use to join your class
  3. Manual addition: Add student information yourself for those who don't have email
  4. Bulk import: Upload a CSV file with multiple students at once
  5. Join code: Share a short code that students can enter on the sign-up page
Students can create their own accounts or you can create accounts for them, depending on your preference.

When your 14-day free trial ends, you'll need to choose a subscription plan to continue using xLearning. You'll receive email reminders before your trial expires, and you can select your plan at any time during or after the trial. If you don't subscribe, your account will become inactive, but your data will be preserved for 30 days in case you decide to subscribe later. After 30 days, your data may be deleted according to our data retention policy.

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings. Go to "Billing & Subscription" and select "Cancel Subscription." You'll still have access to your account until the end of your current billing period. After cancellation, your account will be downgraded to a limited free version where you can access your data but not use premium features. You can reactivate your subscription at any time if you change your mind.

Yes, xLearning allows you to export your data at any time. You can export:
  • Student information (CSV format)
  • Course content and structure (ZIP archive)
  • Assessment results and statistics (CSV or Excel format)
  • Uploaded files and resources (ZIP archive)
  • Communication history (PDF or text format)
We believe in data portability and giving you ownership of your content and information.

Technical Questions

xLearning works in any modern web browser and does not require any special software installation. For optimal performance, we recommend:
  • Web browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest two versions)
  • Internet connection: Minimum 1 Mbps download/upload (5+ Mbps recommended for video)
  • Devices: Computer, tablet, or smartphone with access to a modern web browser
  • For video conferencing: Webcam and microphone
Our mobile apps are available for iOS 12+ and Android 7.0+.

Yes, we take data security very seriously:
  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3
  • Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256
  • We perform regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Our platform complies with GDPR, FERPA, and other privacy regulations
  • We use secure AWS infrastructure with redundant backups
  • Two-factor authentication is available for all accounts
We never sell your data or use it for advertising purposes. You retain ownership of all content you create on the platform.
