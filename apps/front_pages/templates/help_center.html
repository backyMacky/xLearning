Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about LinguaConnect

You can invite students by navigating to the "Students" section in your dashboard and clicking the "Invite Students" button. You can either send individual invitations via email or generate a class code that students can use to join your class directly. The system also allows you to import student lists from CSV files for bulk invitations.

Yes, LinguaConnect allows you to create personalized learning paths for individual students or groups. In the "Curriculum" section, you can design custom learning sequences, assign specific resources, and set unique proficiency goals based on each student's needs and progress level.

LinguaConnect supports multiple ways to incorporate external resources. You can upload files directly to your resource library, embed content from popular platforms like YouTube or Vimeo, link to external websites, or use our integration options with services like Google Drive, Dropbox, and OneDrive to keep your teaching materials organized and accessible.

Our progress tracking system monitors student activities across all learning modules. Teachers can access detailed analytics that show completion rates, time spent on activities, performance on quizzes and assignments, and participation in discussions. Students can also view their own progress through user-friendly dashboards that highlight achievements and areas needing improvement.

LinguaConnect currently supports teaching and learning in over 25 languages, including Spanish, French, German, Mandarin Chinese, Japanese, Arabic, Russian, Italian, Portuguese, and many more. Our platform interface is available in 10 languages, and we're continuously adding support for additional languages based on user requests and global education needs.

LinguaConnect offers multiple feedback options for assignments. You can provide text comments, audio recordings, video feedback, or use our annotation tools for written work. The platform also includes rubric-based assessment tools to streamline grading and ensure consistent evaluation. Students receive notifications when feedback is available and can respond directly through the platform's messaging system.

Yes, LinguaConnect is fully responsive and works on all devices. We also offer dedicated mobile apps for iOS and Android that provide an optimized experience for smartphones and tablets. The mobile version includes all core features of the platform, allowing teachers and students to manage their language learning activities on the go.

To create conversation practice activities, go to the "Activities" section and select "New Conversation Practice." You can set up guided dialogues with prompts, role-playing scenarios, or free-form discussions. Our platform supports both text-based and audio/video conversation formats. You can assign students to pairs or groups, provide preparation materials, and schedule synchronous or asynchronous practice sessions.

LinguaConnect provides several support channels. Our help center contains comprehensive guides and tutorials. For direct assistance, you can contact our support team via email, live chat (available Monday-Friday, 8am-8pm EST), or schedule a video call for more complex issues. Premium and institutional accounts receive priority support with dedicated account managers and faster response times.

In the "Reports" section of your dashboard, you can generate various reports on student performance and activity. All reports can be exported in multiple formats including PDF, Excel, CSV, and Google Sheets. You can schedule regular automated reports or create custom one-time exports. The platform complies with data privacy regulations, ensuring that all exported data is handled securely.
