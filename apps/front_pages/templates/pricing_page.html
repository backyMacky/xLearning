Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about LinguaConnect's pricing and features.

Student count refers to the number of active student profiles you can have in your account at one time. If a student completes their studies, you can archive their profile without affecting your limit. The Solo Teacher plan allows up to 10 active students, the Language Pro plan allows up to 30, and the Language School plan has no limit.

We accept all major credit cards and PayPal. Your subscription will be automatically renewed unless you cancel before the next billing cycle. For annual subscriptions, we'll send a reminder email 14 days before renewal. All payment information is securely handled through our payment processor and is never stored on our servers.

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all plans. If you're not satisfied with LinguaConnect within the first 30 days of your paid subscription, contact our support team and we'll process a full refund. This is in addition to the 14-day free trial available for all new users.
Yes, you can change your plan at any time. When upgrading, the new rate will be prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle. When downgrading, the new rate will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. All your data will be preserved when changing plans, but note that if you downgrade to a plan with lower student limits, you'll need to archive excess student profiles.
