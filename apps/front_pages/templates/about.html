Our Journey

How We Got Here

The story of xLearning's development and growth

Founded
2020

xLearning was founded by a team of language teachers and tech experts who saw a gap in the educational technology market.

Beta Launch
2021

Our first version was launched to a select group of language teachers who provided invaluable feedback for improvement.

1,000 Users
2022

We reached our first major milestone of 1,000 active teachers using the platform to manage their language classes.

EdTech Award
2023

xLearning won the prestigious EdTech Breakthrough Award for "Best Language Learning Platform".

Global Expansion
2024

We expanded to support teachers in over 50 countries and added support for 15 languages.

Today
2025

Now serving over 2,000 language teachers worldwide, with continued innovation and growth on the horizon.
