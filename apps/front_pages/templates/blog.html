Modern Teaching Methodologies

Communicative Language Teaching

Focus on meaningful interaction in authentic contexts, developing students' communicative competence through practical language use rather than rote memorization of grammar rules.

Task-Based Learning

Structure lessons around purposeful tasks that require learners to use language to solve problems, complete projects, or achieve specific outcomes, mimicking real-world language use.

Content and Language Integrated Learning

Teach subject content through the medium of a foreign language, simultaneously developing both subject knowledge and language proficiency in a meaningful context.

Cooperative Language Learning

Facilitate structured group activities that promote peer interaction, collaborative problem-solving, and mutual support, creating a positive interdependence among learners.
