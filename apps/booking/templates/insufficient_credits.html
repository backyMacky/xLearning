About Credits
What are credits?

Credits are the currency used to book sessions with teachers on the Learning Platform. Each credit typically allows you to book one session.

How to get more credits?

You can purchase credits through our secure payment system. We offer various credit packages to suit your needs.

Refund policy

If you cancel a session more than 24 hours before the scheduled time, the credit will be refunded to your account. Late cancellations do not qualify for refunds.
